Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive and user-friendly library management system that helps libraries of all sizes efficiently manage their resources, serve their patrons, and promote literacy and learning in their communities.

About the System

The Library Management System is designed to streamline the day-to-day operations of libraries, including:

  • Book inventory management
  • Member registration and management
  • Book circulation (issuing and returning)
  • Fine calculation and collection
  • Reporting and analytics

Library Rules

  1. Members can borrow up to 3 books at a time.
  2. The loan period for books is 14 days.
  3. A fine of $0.50 per day will be charged for overdue books.
  4. Lost or damaged books must be replaced or paid for.
  5. Membership cards must be presented when borrowing books.
  6. Silence must be maintained in the reading areas.
  7. Food and drinks are not allowed in the library.
